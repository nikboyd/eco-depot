### Narrative Source

[Quote:][narrative]
"When a [truck][truck] arrives at the [loading bay][loading.bay], the clerk enters the manifest accompanying the load and
checks in the [drums][drum] one at a time. As each drum is checked in, it is assigned an identifier."

* the ECO [depot][depot] _contains_ a [loading bay][loading.bay]
* a [truck][truck] _delivers_ a drum load _to_ the [loading bay][loading.bay]
* a drum load _contains_ [drums][drum]
